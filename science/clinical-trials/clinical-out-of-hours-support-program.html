Clinical Out-of-Hours Support Program

To reach Amgen's Clinical Trial Contact Center free of toll charges, dial the number from the list below that matches your location. A representative will be available to assist in responding to urgent after-hour medical inquiries from a HealthCare Professional treating a patient currently enrolled in an Amgen Clinical Trial. For responses to non-urgent inquiries please contact the Clinical Trial Principal Investigator who is best suited to address all questions. After dialing the number below you will receive a prompt to enter an access code. When prompted enter the access code: 2 6 4 3 6 (AMGEN) and you will be connected to a representative supporting this service.
