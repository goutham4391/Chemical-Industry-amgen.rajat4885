PEOPLE & CULTURE 12.14.2021

A Look Back at the Inspiring “People of Amgen” Series on Instagram

Over the past year, a number of colleagues shared their personal stories in the 2021 People of Amgen series on Amgen’s Instagram page, highlighting the human side of Amgen and reflecting the company’s commitment to diversity, inclusion and belonging.

Those who participated shared personal anecdotes about everything from living with an autism diagnosis and managing irritable bowel disease (IBD) to their personal and professional journeys. These stories show how the people of Amgen take responsibility for creating a welcoming, inclusive, and productive work environment where everyone feels valued, regardless of their differences.

We have been inspired by every story and we hope you were too. Click the names below to see them one more time.
