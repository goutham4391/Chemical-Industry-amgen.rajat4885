Scientific Advisory Boards

At Amgen, we recognize that innovation comes from many sources. The formation of the Amgen Scientific Advisory Boards formalizes our belief in engaging outside experts to help us theorize, test and spur our discovery and development processes forward. The Scientific Advisory Boards provide external scientific review of our research and development activities and assist management in making significant scientific judgments.
